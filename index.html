Instructions

Welcome to THICK-THACK-TOE where is this not your typical tic-tac-toe! This game is designed for the player to form a straight line with the symbol assigned to them. The line can be VERTICAL, HORIZONTAL or DIAGONAL. As long as you can connect a line from corner to corner you get a point. FIRST TO 5 POINTS wins the game.

ADDITIONAL INSTRUCTIONS:
1. Whoever creates the first straight line gets 1 POINT.
2. If both players will not manage to get a straight line from corner to corner of the board in either diagonally, vertically or horizontally, it will be a DRAW. 0 POINTS shall be given.
3. There will be no take-away moves.
4. In VS COMPUTER game mode, if the player manages to get a draw the computer will get a point.
